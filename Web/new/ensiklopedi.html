Bacterial Leaf Blight

Gejala : Mulai dari bercak abu-abu hingga kuning pada permukaan daun atau di ujung daun, berlanjut ke semua daun, dan akhirnya daunnya mengering, mati.

Cara memperbaikinya : Hilangkan gulma, jerami dan turiang di luar musim; Disarankan untuk tidak memotong sebagian daun benih padi saat penanaman; Perkembangan penyakit dapat dihambat secara efektif oleh varietas tahan, pemupukan nitrogen, dan reduksi tajuk kelembaban; Penggunaan gen resisten tunggal; Manajemen air

Bacterial Leaf Streak

Gejala: Garis lurus transparan di antara tulang daun; Ukur Lebar 0,5 – 1 mm dan 3-5 mm; Kemudian mengembang dan berubah menjadi hijau tua; Eksudat bakteri terbentuk di permukaan gejala; Kemudian buah menjadi coklat muda, semua daun menjadi pucat, dan putih menjadi abu-abu.

Cara memperbaikinya: Budaya teknis; Varietas tahan bahan kimia; perlakuan benih; Jerami dan turiang yang sudah dipakai sebaiknya dihilangkan untuk menghindari penyebaran penyakit.

Bacterial Panicle Blight

Gejala: Dapat dilihat pada planlet, pelepah daun bendera, dan seterusnya; Spikelet yang terinfeksi berwarna seperti jerami dan memiliki perubahan warna gabah yang busuk dan malai yang kosong; Dibawah kondisi ideal bakteri berkembang biak dengan cepat, gejala mulai muncul 3 hari setelah inokulasi malai dengan bertahap peningkatan jumlah malai yang terserang.

How to fix it: Apply the correct dosage of active ingredients, keeping in mind the quantity and quality of the product; Apply the product at the right time, as it has preventive measures against the appearance of symptoms; Introducing the Integrated Management Program to control B. glumae

Blast Disease

Symptom: On the leaves there are rhombus-shaped spots in the middle and tapered at both ends; The size of the spot is about 1 - 1.5 - 0.3 - 0.5 cm and develops a gray color in the middle; The infected panicle neck will turn blackish and break.

How to fix it: Use blast resistant varieties alternately; Use nitrogen fertilizer as recommended; Strive for the right planting time, so that the initial time of flowering is not a lot of dew and rain continuously - Use a function or phosdiphene and kaugamin

Brown Spot Disease

Symptom: Brownish spots the size of a sesame seed on the leaves, midrib, and grain; Early or undeveloped symptoms are small, and circular, dark brown or purplish brown in color; Fully developed spots will be brown with a whitish center; Leaves with lots of spots will wither, dry and die; In severe cases blackish brown or dark brown patches develop on the calyx, coleoptile, panicle and young roots.

How to fix it: Good fertilizer management; Use of potassium; Seed treatment with kaptan, thiram, chitosan, carbendzim, or mancozeb; Spraying mancozeb – tricyclazole at the tillering and pregnant stages.

Deadheart Disease

Symptom: The death of rice plant shoots in the vegetative stage; Rice panicles look white and empty at the generative stage.

How to fix it: Can be done by using mechanics, biological agents and insecticides; If it is found that the intensity of the rice stem borer attack has reached the control threshold, use pesticides in a systematic way; The use of insecticides can use the active ingredients of carbofuran, benzene, bisultap, carbonsulfan, dimehipa, amitraz or fipronil.

Hispa Disease

Symptom: There is a pattern of white and parallel lines along the main axis of the leaf; In severe attacks, the white spots get bigger every year; The appearance of insects on damaged leaves, generally on the upper side; The leaves that are attacked by these insects will dry up; Then land that has been badly damaged will look like it's on fire

How to fix it : Use closer spacing with greater leaf density which can tolerate a greater amount of hispa; Plant crops early in the season to avoid peak populations; Cut the tips of the shoots to prevent insects from laying eggs; Collect adult insects with a sweeper net, especially in the morning; Remove all kinds of weeds from the fields during the no-planting season; Avoid excessive nitrogen fertilization in affected fields; Do a crop rotation to break the life cycle of this pest.

Tungro Disease

Symptom : Discoloration of the young leaves of rice plants that turn yellow to orange; The leaves also look twisted.

How to fix it : Planting rice simultaneously on an area of ​​at least 25 hectare; Set the rice planting time as well as possible. At the peak of population density of green leafhoppers, rice plants were more than 45 days after planting, the age of rice plants that were not susceptible to tungro disease.
